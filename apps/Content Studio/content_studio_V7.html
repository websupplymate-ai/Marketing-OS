Manual publishing Free · default
No paid publishing API needed. "Publish" on a queue post: marks it published for tracking, copies the caption to your clipboard, and gives you one-click buttons to the right upload page per channel (Instagram, YouTube Studio, LinkedIn). Your media's Drive link sits on the post — open, download, upload, paste caption, done.
Why not direct platform keys? Browser apps can't post directly: LinkedIn blocks browser API calls entirely, YouTube uploads require an OAuth login flow (not an API key), and Instagram needs a Meta Business app token. Aggregators like Ayrshare exist to bridge exactly that — which is why the free path here is a fast manual flow instead.
